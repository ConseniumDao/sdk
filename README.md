## Overview

**Consenium OS** is a decentralized operating layer designed to give individuals full control over how their personal data is accessed, used, and monetized in the AI ecosystem.

As artificial intelligence systems increasingly rely on user-generated data, Consenium OS introduces a transparent framework where users can:

- Track which AI systems access their data
- Manage permissions across applications
- Monetize data contributions
- Maintain full ownership of their digital identity

Consenium OS empowers users to transform their **data footprint into a programmable asset**.

## Key Features

### Data Visibility
Monitor how your data is used by AI models and digital services.

### Permission Control
Grant, revoke, or customize access to your data across applications.

### Data Monetization
Earn rewards when AI models utilize your data for training or inference.

### Privacy-First Architecture
Encrypted identity layers ensure users remain in control of their digital footprint.

### Decentralized Infrastructure
Built to integrate with decentralized identity and blockchain networks.

## Why Consenium OS?

The AI economy is powered by user data, yet most individuals have **no visibility or control** over how their data is used.

Consenium OS changes that.

It creates a new paradigm where:

- Data ownership belongs to users
- AI access is permissioned
- Value flows back to contributors
